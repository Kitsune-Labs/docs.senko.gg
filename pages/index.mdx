## What or who is Senko?

Senko is a Discord bot that is designed to be configurable while still being easy to use. It is written in TypeScript and uses the [discord.js](https://discord.js.org) library.

Senko is based from the [anime](https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GR4PXWK1Y/the-helpful-fox-senko-san) and [manga](https://mangadex.org/title/c26269c7-0f5d-4966-8cd5-b79acb86fb7a/sewayaki-kitsune-no-senko-san) Sewayaki kitsune no Senko-san created by [Rimukoro](https://twitter.com/rimukoro).
